About

I’m a British artist and art therapist and I have been based in Madrid for the past 20 years. I work from a studio in the north of Madrid where I also run a private art therapy clinic. More than just providing a showcase of my work, my aim here is to communicate something about my creative environment, my inspiration and my working methods both as an artist and an art therapist.
